# Test Route Recording Matrix
## Location Parameters
- Starting Point: Main Street & 1st Avenue
- Block Dimensions: Approximately 0.25 miles per side
- Total Route Length: 1 mile per lap
- Time per Lap: 3-5 minutes
- Traffic Conditions: Light to moderate

## Lap 1: Baseline Recording
### Purpose: Establish standard driving behavior

**Segment 1 - Initial Straight**
GPS: [Starting Point] to [Corner 1]
Speed: 25 mph steady
Voice Guidance:

"Starting baseline recording. Maintain 25 mph"
"Approaching first right turn in 200 feet"
"Prepare for standard right turn at 15 mph"

**Segment 2 - First Turn**
GPS: [Corner 1] turning right
Speed: Reduce to 15 mph
Voice Guidance:

"Begin turn at 15 mph"
"Apply steady steering input"
"Maintain consistent radius through turn"

**Segment 3 - Second Straight**
GPS: [Corner 1] to [Corner 2]
Speed: Resume 25 mph
Voice Guidance:

"Accelerate smoothly to 25 mph"
"Maintain center lane position"
"Left turn approaching in quarter mile"

**Segment 4 - Second Turn**
GPS: [Corner 2] turning left
Speed: Reduce to 15 mph
Voice Guidance:

"Reduce speed for left turn"
"Begin turn at 15 mph"
"Complete turn and prepare to accelerate"

## Lap 2: Dynamic Speed Variations
### Purpose: Capture different acceleration and braking patterns

**Segment 1 - Quick Acceleration**
GPS: [Starting Point] to [Corner 1]
Speed: 0-35 mph
Voice Guidance:

"Perform moderate acceleration to 35 mph"
"Hold speed steady at 35"
"Prepare for braking sequence"

**Segment 2 - Performance Turn**
GPS: [Corner 1] turning right
Speed: 20 mph through turn
Voice Guidance:

"Brake firmly to 20 mph"
"Maintain higher turn speed"
"Apply smooth steering input"

**Segment 3 - Variable Speed Straight**
GPS: [Corner 1] to [Corner 2]
Speed: 35-25-35 mph
Voice Guidance:

"Accelerate to 35 mph"
"Reduce to 25 mph over 5 seconds"
"Return to 35 mph smoothly"

## Lap 3: Alternative Route Variations
### Purpose: Capture different turning radiuses and road positions

**Segment 1 - Wide Turn Entry**
GPS: [Starting Point] to [Corner 1 Wide]
Speed: 15 mph
Voice Guidance:

"Position vehicle for wide turn"
"Begin turn from outer lane"
"Maintain steady 15 mph through turn"

**Segment 2 - Side Street Navigation**
GPS: [Side Street] to [Corner 2 Sharp]
Speed: 20 mph
Voice Guidance:

"Navigate narrow street section"
"Watch for parked vehicles"
"Prepare for sharp turn ahead"

**Segment 3 - Sharp Turn Exit**
GPS: [Corner 2 Sharp] to [Main Street]
Speed: 12 mph
Voice Guidance:

"Execute sharp turn at 12 mph"
"Apply greater steering input"
"Straighten and accelerate smoothly"

Road surface texture
Ambient and engine sound
