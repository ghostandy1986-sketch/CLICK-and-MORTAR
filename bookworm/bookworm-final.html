Mindfull

By King@ndy

Lured from the depths of depression by a single ember of inspiration, one man makes his final attempt at happiness — armed with nothing but a backpack full of poems and the desperate desire for one person to feel what he felt writing them.

"The notebook is all he loves. All he has. A new hunger rises inside the burn: to share it."

Raw, poetic, and fiercely alive — Mindfull is a story about climbing back, finding your voice, and guarding your mind like the garden it is.

Preview: Mindfull
Opening Pages
The sun's rays claw through endless black, thinner with every inch.

Life-giver, day-maker, night-breaker — now a fading ember, cursed by the very shadows it cannot burn away.

Its glow shrinks to a dying whisper, just enough to carve these words into the void. Deeper it reaches.
Into the pit where nothing lives.

A road that ends in nothing. A place light has no business.

Yet the sun pushes on — not for hope, not for purpose, but because a debt lingers, a favor owed to a dear friend...
