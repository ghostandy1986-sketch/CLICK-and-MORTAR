Community Guidelines

Our Promise to You

SafeSpace exists to provide genuine support and healing. We are committed to:

  • Maintaining your complete anonymity
  • Providing professional moderation 24/7
  • Connecting you with real mental health resources
  • Creating a judgment-free environment

Community Standards

  • Treat everyone with kindness and respect
  • No judgment, criticism, or minimizing others' experiences
  • Focus on support, encouragement, and healing
  • Respect privacy and confidentiality
  • Report concerning content to our trained moderators

Safety First

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please contact emergency services or use our crisis resources. Our platform is for support, not crisis intervention.
